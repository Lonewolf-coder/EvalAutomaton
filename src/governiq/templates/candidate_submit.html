What Happens After You Submit

Your bot export is parsed
We read the JSON to understand what dialogs, entities, service nodes, and FAQs your bot contains.
Structural checks run automatically
We verify that all required components exist: correct dialog names, entity nodes, Agent Node, POST/GET/PUT/DELETE service calls, FAQ entries.
Live conversation tests (if webhook provided)
An AI driver talks to your bot, tests booking, retrieval, modification, cancellation, and edge cases.
You get a detailed report
Every check is explained in plain English. You see exactly what passed, what failed, and what to fix.

Checklist Before Submitting

Bot export is a valid JSON file
Export from Kore.ai, not a screenshot or PDF.
DialogGPT is enabled
Go to Bot Settings > Intelligence > DialogGPT and make sure it's ON before exporting.
All dialogs are published
Unpublished dialogs will not appear in the export.
FAQs have alternate questions
Each FAQ should have at least 2 alternate phrasings.
